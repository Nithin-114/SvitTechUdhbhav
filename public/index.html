ABOUT THE EVENT

Techno Udbhav is the Annual Technical Extravaganza hosted by Swami Vivekanand Institute of Technology (SVIT). This vibrant fest is a melting pot of innovation, creativity, and technological prowess, where students from diverse colleges showcase their talents and compete in a variety of events. From coding competitions and robotics challenges to workshops on emerging technologies and captivating guest lectures. Techno Udbhav offers a platform for students to learn, grow, and network with industry professionals.
